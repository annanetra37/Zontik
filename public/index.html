About Zoncik

We believe local businesses deserve the spotlight

Zoncik was built with a simple idea: small and medium businesses are the backbone of every community, yet they're often the hardest to find online.

We created a space where SMEs can be discovered without competing against giant ad budgets — and where people can find authentic, local alternatives to big-box brands. No algorithms. No paid placements. Just great businesses, listed fairly.

Our Story

We believe small businesses deserve a big spotlight

Zoncik was born out of a simple frustration — great local businesses were invisible online, buried under big brands and paid ads. We set out to change that.

Today Zoncik is a free, community-driven directory where small and medium businesses can be discovered by the people who matter most: their neighbours, their city, their community.
